Description

Lasagna is an Italian recipe consisting of sheets of pasta with tomato sauce and some kind of filling. The filling is usually cheese and/or meat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Onion and garlic
  • Creamed milk
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into cubes.
  2. Cook the chicken with onion and garlic.
  3. Add the creamed milk, ketchup and mustard and mix.
  4. Serve with rice and fries.
