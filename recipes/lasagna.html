Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian recipe consisting of sheets of pasta with tomato sauce and some kind of filling. The filling is usually cheese and/or meat.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Layer the lasagna with the noodles, meat sauce and cheese.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
